Birds In Our Backyard

Black Capped Chickadee
  • Chickadees will sing to their young when feeding them.

  • Male Chickadees do not sing unless they are away from other male birds

  • When nesting, Chickadees will find a hole in a tree, usually 3 to 21 feet off the ground.

  • Chickadees will hide seeds in many different places that only they know about, and can remember thousands of these locations.
